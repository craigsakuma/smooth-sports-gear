HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
======================

DROP YOUR PHOTOS INTO THESE FOLDERS AND THEY WILL APPEAR ON THE WEBSITE.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRODUCT PHOTOS  →  images/products/
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name your files exactly as shown:

  jerseys.jpg       → Jerseys & Uniforms card
  tshirts.jpg       → T-Shirts & Polos card
  hoodies.jpg       → Hoodies & Sweatshirts card
  hats.jpg          → Hats & Caps card
  stickers.jpg      → Stickers & Decals card
  mugs.jpg          → Mugs & Drinkware card
  posters.jpg       → Posters & Banners card
  signs.jpg         → Signs & Displays card

------------------------------------------------------------------------
GALLERY PHOTOS  →  images/gallery/
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name your files exactly as shown (up to 8 photos):

  01.jpg   → Large feature photo (use your best one — it appears biggest)
  02.jpg
  03.jpg
  04.jpg
  05.jpg   → Tall photo (appears taller than the others)
  06.jpg
  07.jpg
  08.jpg

------------------------------------------------------------------------
YOUR PERSONAL PHOTOS  →  images/
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  owner.jpg    → Your photo in the "Our Story" section
  team.jpg     → A team photo that appears beside your story

------------------------------------------------------------------------
TIPS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Photos should be JPG or WEBP format
- Landscape photos (wider than tall) work best for products and gallery
- For best quality: aim for at least 800px wide
- File names must be LOWERCASE and match exactly (no capital letters)
- After adding photos, you must update index.html to show them.
  Find the comment that says "Replace with:" above each image area
  and follow the instructions there.
