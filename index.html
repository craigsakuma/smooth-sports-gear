Our Story

From Coach
to Community

It started with a problem every coach knows: getting your team into sharp, matching uniforms without the headaches.

I've coached youth sports for as long as I can remember. One season, fed up with poor quality, slow delivery, and generic designs, I decided to figure it out myself. I took a design course, learned the craft, and by the next season my team looked better than everyone else on the field.

Word got around fast. Other coaches started asking. Then parents. Then entire programs. What started as a personal solution became a community service.

"When a kid puts on a uniform that fits right and looks sharp, they stand up straighter. They play harder. That's why I do this."

Thirty years later, I've made gear for little leagues, high schools, rec leagues, and traveling teams. I've outfitted first-time players and varsity athletes. I've seen uniforms worn with pride long after the final whistle.

Now I'm ready to bring that same care and quality to communities beyond our own. If you're within a couple hours' drive, I'd love to outfit your team.

1994 Made first uniforms for my own coaching team
1998 Began outfitting other local teams
2005 Expanded to serve the entire community
Today Growing to serve neighboring communities
